    Banh Mi Thit

    Flavorful Vietnamese Delight

    A vibrant Vietnamese sandwich featuring a harmonious blend of cold cuts, pickled daikon & carrots, cucumber, cilantro, jalapeno, and house aioli. A delightful combination of flavors and textures that bring a taste of Vietnam to your plate.

    Beef Pho

    Hearty Aromatic Beef Noodle Soup

    Immerse yourself in the rich essence of Vietnamese cuisine with this dish. House beef stock forms the base for this aromatic soup, complemented by rice noodles, tender brisket, thinly sliced ribeye, and flavorful meatballs. A hearty and comforting experience.

    Banh Mi Op La + Cha

    Fusion Egg and Ham Sandwich

    Enjoy a fusion of flavors with this creation. Fried eggs and ham join forces with pickled daikon & carrots, cucumber, cilantro, jalapeno, and house aioli. The marriage of these ingredients offers a satisfying balance of savory and tangy notes.

    Chicken Pho

    Nourishing Chicken Rice Noodle Soup

    Savor the essence of traditional Vietnamese flavors in this dish. Free-range chicken stock forms the backdrop for a bowl of rice noodles and succulent chicken. The result is a soothing and nourishing bowl of goodness.

    Vietnamese Crepe

    Crispy Protein-Filled Delight

    Embark on a culinary adventure with this crispy battered crepe. Your choice of protein is paired with bean sprouts and onions, encased within the delectable crepe. A delightful medley of textures and tastes that captures the essence of Vietnamese cuisine.
